Thrift

approved by Team .EXE, NIT-H and The Faculty, NIT-H

To create an online platform that allows users to buy and sell second-hand books, as well as donate books to NGOs in their local community. The platform will have two main functions: an online bookstore and a donation feature. The online bookstore will allow users to create an account and upload listings of the second-hand books they want to sell. Customers can browse the books by category, author, or title and make purchases through a secure payment gateway. The donation feature will allow users to donate their used books to NGOs in their local community. This will help promote reading among underprivileged communities and reduce waste by giving books a second life. The platform will have a list of partnering NGOs and their contact information. Users can choose an NGO they want to donate to and arrange for pickup or drop-off of their donated books.
Our project will not only provide an affordable option for people to purchase books but also make it easy for people to donate books to NGOs near them. This initiative will help promote literacy and education, as well as encourage sustainability by giving books a second chance at life.
